How it works

Three simple steps. You nominate, they apply, and if accepted, you both win.

1

Nominate someone

Go to the Nominate section and submit their name, LinkedIn profile and email. That's it.

2

They complete their application

Your nominee receives an invitation to fill in their application and join the community.

3

They become a member

Once approved, they're in. You've just helped expand the community with someone you trust.

Two ways to refer

Choose whichever works best for you. Both work on web and mobile.

Fill in the form

Enter their first name, last name, LinkedIn URL and email directly in the Nominate section. Quick and simple.

Share your link

Copy your personal nomination link and share it with the person you want to invite. They fill in their own details.
